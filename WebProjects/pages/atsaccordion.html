How long does the process Take?

Our training is divided into 3 different levels:

ATS Level X - 5 Months: Introductory Robotics lessons and training

ATS Level Y - 8 Months: Advanced Robotics and Automation Engineering training sessions with projects.

ATS Level Z - 12 Months: Advanced and more complex projects, access to internships, mentorships and competitions.
